Future work — pathway-hierarchical encoder (H-JEPA / HRM-style)

Replace the flat gene-token encoder with two attention levels: a low level over gene tokens and an abstract level over pathway tokens, wired by the Hallmark membership (a gene connects only to the pathways it belongs to). The biology becomes an inductive bias, not just a loss.

▲ abstract level — pathway tokens
self-attention across programs (reasoning over pathways)
self-attention across genes (local syntax)
▼ gene level — gene tokens
\(z\) — cell latent  pooled from pathway tokens
Inductive bias = biological sparsity: genes route up to their pathways (↑ pool by membership), pathways send context back down (↓ modulate). Two abstraction levels — like Hierarchical JEPA / HRM — instead of dense all-to-all attention.
