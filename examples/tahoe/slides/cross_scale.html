Vision — a hierarchical, cross-scale world model of biology

Biology is multi-scale. Foundation models exist per scale (molecules, sequences, cells, tissues) but don't bridge them. A hierarchical JEPA world model (à la LeCun) predicts at each level under an action; lower scales inform higher ones and vice-versa (cross-attention compression, à la Xpressor).
