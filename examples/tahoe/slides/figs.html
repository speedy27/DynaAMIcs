\(\mathcal{L}_{\text{sig}}\) — drug signature

Pulls the predicted shift \(\hat z_{\text{pert}}-z_{\text{ctrl}}\) of same-drug cells to the same direction (green), apart from other drugs (red) ⇒ captures drug-specific biology.

\(\mathcal{L}_{\text{path}}\) — pathway coherence

Matches pairwise latent distances to gene-program (module) activity distances ⇒ embeds coordinated pathway structure, not batch noise.
